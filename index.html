Takamatsu Tomori (Vo.)

About

A first-year high school student known as "Haneoka's Weirdo Girl" due to her peculiar personality of collecting all sorts of just-the-right-size things.

Characteristics

  • Lead vocal of the all girl band group MyGO!!!!!.
  • She likes konpeitos because they are small and they look a bit like stars.
  • She often goes to planetariums and aquariums as a way to calm down when she's bothered or worried about something.
  • She likes collecting all sorts of things, such as band-aids, slippery things, just-the-right-size things, and even rocks and fallen leaves that she picks up from the ground.

Character and the real life band of MyGo!!!!!.
